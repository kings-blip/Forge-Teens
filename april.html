THE MONTH OF PASSOVER

This month focuses on the profound theme of **Passover** — a powerful biblical picture of redemption, deliverance, and God's faithfulness to His people. We'll explore how the Passover in the Old Testament points forward to Jesus as our ultimate Passover Lamb, and what this means for our lives today.

Key ideas we'll cover include:

  • The historical and spiritual significance of the Passover feast (Exodus 12)
  • Jesus as the fulfillment of Passover (1 Corinthians 5:7 – "Christ, our Passover lamb, has been sacrificed")
  • Deliverance from bondage, the blood of protection, and new beginnings
  • Practical application: Living in the freedom Christ purchased for us

More details, weekly studies, challenges, and resources will be added here as the month approaches. In the meantime, continue with the 9-Month New Testament Reading Plan and stay connected during Friday calls for discussions and testimonies.
