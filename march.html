MARCH

The Month of Discipline

This month, the teens will explore the truth that love requires discipline. Throughout this quarter, they have been building a deeper understanding of who Christ is. As we prepare for the new quarter, where they will learn how to begin and strengthen a personal relationship with Jesus, we want them to see that walking with the Lord calls for intentional discipline of the body and mind.

This discipline will also apply to their academic lives, especially as they approach the exam season.

Weekly Bible Study Breakdown

Week 1 – March 6th

Discipline

Key Verses: 1 Corinthians 9:24-27

Leader: Sister Emike

Teens in Charge: Kings

Activity: Teens should pray between the hours of 8:40pm to 9pm each day.

Week 2 – March 13th

Delayed Gratification / Waiting

Key Verses: Psalm 62:5

Leader: Bro. Ovbokhan

Teens in Charge: Mercy

Activity: Teens to fast at once twice this week. Shortest is 6-3pm.

Week 3 – March 20th

Creating Time

Leader: Sister Bola

Teens in Charge: Nosa

Activity: Teens to pick a topic in school that they do not like. They are to devote 30 minutes to it each day and drop new insight on the topic in the WhatsApp group during the week. Instructors to also participate.

Week 4 – March 27th

What to do when Self Discipline is Hard

Key Verses: 1 Peter 1:3-9

Leader: Sister Orode

Teens in Charge: Mildred

Activity: Teens to study the life of a selected character of the bible throughout the week. Characters will be picked by ballot. They are to search everywhere in the bible for the character and even history. Research should start dropping from Wednesday and not before that.
