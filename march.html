MARCH

THE MONTH OF DISCIPLINE

This month, the teens will explore the truth that love requires discipline. Throughout this quarter, they have been building a deeper understanding of who Christ is. As we prepare for the new quarter, where they will learn how to begin and strengthen a personal relationship with Jesus, we want them to see that walking with the Lord calls for intentional discipline of the body and mind.

This discipline will also apply to their academic lives, especially as they approach the exam season.

Weekly Challenges & Instructors

Week 1 (March 6th)

Instructor: Sister Bola

Challenge: Teens should pray between the hours of 8:40pm to 9pm each day (20 minutes focused prayer)

Focus: Discipline

Week 2 (March 13th)

Instructor: Bro. Ovbokhan

Challenge: Teens to fast at least twice this week (shortest is 6am-3pm)

Focus: Delayed gratification

Week 3 (March 20th)

Instructor: Sister Emike

Challenge: Teens to pick a topic in school that they do not like. They are to devote 30 minutes to it each day and drop new insight on the topic in the WhatsApp group during the week. Instructors to also participate.

Focus: Creating Time

Week 4 (March 27th)

Instructor: Sister Orode

Challenge: Teens to study the life of a selected character of the Bible throughout the week. Characters will be picked by ballot. They are to search everywhere in the Bible for the character and even history. Research should start dropping from Wednesday and not before that.

Focus: What to do when Self discipline is hard

Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishment. Let's commit to these challenges together and grow stronger in faith and character this month.
