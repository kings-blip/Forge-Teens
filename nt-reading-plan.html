9-Month New Testament Reading Plan

Read through the entire New Testament in ~260 days (approximately one chapter per day). This plan runs alongside your monthly themed Bible studies to help every teen become deeply rooted in the life, teachings, death, resurrection, and ongoing mission of Jesus and the early church.

Start anytime — track progress during Friday calls and share what God is showing you!
