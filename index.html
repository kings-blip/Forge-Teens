Focus for the Year

This year, we really get into the Bible. We will explore various themes of the bible and really become people of the word.

In February, we will be following a bible study plan. The teens will be encouraged to really reason out what they study, ask questions and debate.

Instructors will have a huge role to play this year in terms of accountability, mentorship and involvement in the bible study themselves. We encourage all instructors and Volunteers to study what the teens study so we have a more indepth view of the word.

We will also make good use of peer accountability amongst the teens.

This page is a one-in-all page. It will be reviewed from time to time depending on what is happening in the life of our church. And all content will be viewed for the year from this one link.
