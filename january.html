JANUARY

THE MONTH OF PROVERBS

The goal is for every teen to complete the book of Proverbs by January 31st. We’re starting the year with a strong focus on wisdom.

Throughout this month, Bible study will be more practical and applied. We want the teens to understand that knowledge only becomes wisdom when they put it into action. If ever there is a need for biblical wisdom, 2026 is that year.

Each day in January will focus on one chapter of Proverbs. Every Friday, we’ll reflect on and summarize the chapters read that week, followed by a weekly challenge or practical action to help them live out what they’ve learned.

Teens will also be divided into accountability groups headed by a peer for this month. A champion team will emerge at the end of the month.
