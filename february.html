FEBRUARY

THE MONTH OF LOVE

This month, our focus is on the ultimate love: how it shapes not only our salvation but the way we live each day. Love lies at the very heart of the Christian faith. We won’t get lost in the fluff; instead, we’ll explore the framework that forms our relationship with God and with others.

We’ll also dive into topics like love, trust, and boundaries, and examine the pitfalls of pseudo-love.

February will also be the return of teen-led study. There will be rotations with the Instructors as well to listen in on the discussions and provide insights.
